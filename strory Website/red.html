RED

RED by Ramsey Shehadeh is about the impact on a young man of the disappearance of his sister, and the journey he must go on to find her again. The protagonist finds himself drawn into the world of their favourite board game in a desperate attempt to find her. Ramsey Shehadeh is famous for his unconventional fiction, and this story is a must-read for all his fans.
