ANANDA THE WISE

Years ago when things were calm, there lived a young woman who happens to be a prostitute named armarpali. Every kingdom she goes , the tide of lust hits the kingdom, kings were seduced, queens were jealous ,children were spoiled she got so rich that she could buy kingdoms for herself. Though this lust inflicting woman had a beautiful body Indeed had a beautiful soul too. Despite having all the Gold and every eyes on her .She wanted love and respect from others .On one fine evening there arrived a group of monks led by buddha himself, all of them had a calm presence , an aura that is so quiet to feel . Through the window, armarpali gazed a Young monk who had charm like none other, a charm like a river that hasn’t moved since a long time. Young monk’s name was Ananda. She approached him, talked with him and intention behind the arrival of monks was stay in the village for 4 months because of monsoon. Armarpali in desperation asked Ananda to stay in her house for this monsoon. The wise ananda told her he will ask permission from his mentor buddha, “Master, There is an invitation I got ,there is a woman who wants To keep me in her house” without a second thought buddha allowed him to stay in that dirty woman’s house. Shocked were others “how can a monk stay in a prostitute’s house Buddha said he didn’t mentioned that woman as a prostitute, he respect her . The rain started so did her lust for young monk .She made food for him, she made bed for him, she danced for him and he paid close attention to her the entire time and the rains finally stops. Young ananda went back to buddha, every monk was amazed because ananda brought a female monk with him.
